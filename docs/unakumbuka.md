# Unakumbuka

**Digital memorial platform preserving stories and legacy.**

## Overview
Families want a lasting, shareable place to remember loved ones and pass on their stories.
Unakumbuka provides a dignified digital memorial where memories are preserved and shared.

## What it does
- Create and share digital memorials
- Preserve stories, tributes and legacy in one place
- A calm, respectful experience for families and communities
