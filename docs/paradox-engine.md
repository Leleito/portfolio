# The Paradox Engine

**Harnessing tension to transform your life and the world.**

## Overview
The Paradox Engine is a publishing and ideas platform built around the theme of turning tension
and paradox into personal and collective transformation.

## What it does
- Presents the core ideas and themes of the work
- A clean reading and discovery experience for the audience
