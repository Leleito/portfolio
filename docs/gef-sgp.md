# GEF-SGP Kenya

**Digital platform for the GEF Small Grants Programme in Kenya.**

## Overview
The Global Environment Facility's Small Grants Programme funds community-led environmental
projects. This platform gives the programme a clear public presence and a structured way to
present its work and reach applicants.

## What it does
- Public-facing home for the Small Grants Programme in Kenya
- Presents funded community environmental initiatives and programme information
- Accessible entry point for communities and stakeholders

## Stack
TypeScript · Next.js · Tailwind CSS · Vercel
