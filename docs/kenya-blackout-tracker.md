# Kenya Blackout Tracker

**Crowdsourced power-outage tracking & grid-reliability data for Kenya.**

## Overview
Power reliability in Kenya is hard to see clearly because outage data is scattered and
anecdotal. The Kenya Blackout Tracker crowdsources outage reports into a public picture of
grid reliability over time and place.

## What it does
- Crowdsourced reporting of power outages across Kenya
- Reliability trends by area to make grid performance visible
- Open data to inform citizens, planners and researchers

## Status
Launching at **Power4.africa**.
