# Parsifal / WorldMonitor

**Real-time global intelligence & risk-monitoring dashboard.**

## Overview
Decision-makers need a fast, consolidated view of world events and emerging risk instead of
scanning dozens of sources. Parsifal aggregates and presents global signals in a single
real-time dashboard.

## What it does
- Real-time monitoring of global events and developments
- Consolidated risk and intelligence views for situational awareness
- Clean, focused dashboard designed for quick reading

## Stack
TypeScript · Next.js · Vercel
