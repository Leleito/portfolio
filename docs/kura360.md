# KURA360

**Campaign compliance & operations platform for Kenyan elections.**

## Overview
Political campaigns in Kenya must manage contributions, spending and reporting under strict
electoral rules, usually across spreadsheets and disconnected tools. KURA360 gives campaign
teams a single operations platform to run day-to-day activity while staying compliant.

## What it does
- Campaign operations and team coordination in one workspace
- Contribution and expenditure tracking aligned to compliance reporting
- Structured records that stand up to audit and oversight requirements

## Stack
TypeScript · Next.js · Tailwind CSS · Vercel
